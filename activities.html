We at the Dehiwala Zoo are always up to providing entertaintment and practical knowledge to our visitors. Therefore for a ever lasting life experiences, we have avtivities for you to be involved as well. Under the extreme care, guide and surveillance we have arranged these activities for you to experience and have the maximum out of your visit to the Dehiwala Zoo.
So buckle up and don't hesitate to take part in these wonderful activities to create memories of you sharing your time with us and these fantastic creatures.

1. Dance with Elephants

Sri Lanka is a country that is highly bonded with elephants and has had no troubles in taming them for years.
Under the extreme care and handling of our highly skillful elephant trainers and tamers, this is the perfect opportunity to interact with the fascinatingly large mammal and do something extraordinary.
The elephants available for this activity, are trained specially to interact and dance with strangers and it nods it head and wavers to the music.

So don't worry and just dance to the tune!!!

  1. Location: Open Space (Block C)

  2. Time: 4pm-6pm

  3. Price: Rs. 500 per pax for 10 minutes

2. Yala National Park Tour

The Yala National Park isn't directly linked with the zoo but arrangements can be made.
Contact our office to tour one of the biggest Wildlife habitats of Sri Lanka situated in the South of the country.
Explore the entire jungle on jeeps along with your family and friends and spend the entire day seperately from morning to evening.

LET THE EXPLORATIONS BEGIN!!!

  1. Location: Palatupana, Yala, Sri Lanka

  2. Time: 9am-4pm

  3. Price: Rs. 3500 per jeep. (Can accomodate upto 7 people).

3. Feed the Cuties

The activity available at present which has the longest queue due to its loving nature.
Experience the activity of hand feeding milk to farm animals through bottles as if its own mother who is feeding it.
Fondle these cute animals and recieve their love back as you give your love to them.

BE THAT LOVING PARENT!!!

  1. Location: Farmyard (Block L)

  2. Time: 10am-12pm

  3. Price: Rs. 200 per bottle
